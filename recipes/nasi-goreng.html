Nasi Goreng

A famous Indonesian fried rice dish with eggs, vegetables, and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old rice is best)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g chicken (diced)
  • 1 green onion (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook until golden brown.
  3. Push the chicken to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs.
  4. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, and sweet soy sauce.
  5. Mix everything well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with chopped green onions and serve hot.
