Sate Ayam

Grilled chicken skewers served with peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast (cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce, honey, garlic powder, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  2. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  3. Grill over medium heat until fully cooked.
  4. Serve with peanut sauce.
