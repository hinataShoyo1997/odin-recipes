Rendang

A traditional Indonesian dish made with slow-cooked beef in coconut milk and spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 5 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp chili paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut beef into cubes and set aside.
  2. In a pan, saut√© garlic and lemongrass until fragrant.
  3. Add beef and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, bay leaves, and chili paste.
  5. Simmer for 2-3 hours until beef is tender and sauce thickens.
